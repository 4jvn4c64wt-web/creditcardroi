Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I suggest new features or cards?
Send your ideas to . Whether it's a card you want supported, a feature you'd find useful, or anything else, your feedback shapes what gets built next.
Is my data safe?
Your data never leaves your browser - no servers or uploads. Everything runs locally.
What banks work with this?
Any bank or budgeting app that lets you export a CSV. The tracker auto-detects the format.
How is this different from real-time card optimization tools?
Those tools help you maximize future purchases by suggesting which card to use at checkout or auto-applying offers. We do something different: we analyze past transaction data to tell you how much value you got from each card and whether the annual fee was worth it.
Do I need to use this regularly?
No. Many people use it just once or twice a year at renewal time. You're also welcome to review your card performance more frequently. You can easily see which credits you haven't used yet.
Can I use this for cards you don't support yet?
Not yet, but new cards are added regularly. Reach out to and let us know which card you'd like to see next.
Do you have a privacy policy?
We don't collect your transaction data, period. Everything runs locally in your browser. Future Pro users will need to verify a license key online, and Cloudflare handles basic site hosting and analytics. That's it.
How accurate is the categorization?
The tracker uses a rule-based engine with hundreds of known merchants. When it doesn't recognize a merchant, it looks at other details embedded in the transaction to classify it. If it's still unsure, it defaults safely and flags it for your review. Pro users will be able to add their own merchant rules to improve accuracy over time.
Can I map more than one card number to a single card?
Yes. This is useful if your card was lost, stolen, or reissued with a new number. You can map multiple card numbers to the same rewards card so all transactions count toward that card's totals.
Can I use this on my phone?
Yes. It runs in any modern browser, including mobile.
What happens if I clear my browser?
Because the tracker stores everything in your browser's local storage, your data will be cleared too. Export a JSON backup before clearing your browser and upload it instead of a CSV next time you boot up the tracker. The backup file will restore all your data and preferences.
